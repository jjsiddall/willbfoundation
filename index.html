Team Will B. was featured in the 7/14 "Great Strides" Newsletter:

This month we are featuring the Belmonte family for their outstanding commitment to finding a cure for cystic fibrosis. On July 14, Bill Belmonte Sr. and Bob Ferrari of the Will B. Foundation along with their sponsorship partner, Chicago Prime Steakhouse hosted their Inaugural Golf Outing at Lake Barrington Shores Country Club in Lake Barrington, IL.

Over 75 golfers attended the big day including a few pros who helped fundraise for the event. All in all, the event has raised over $4,600 and they're not even done yet!

Fighting CF

It'd be easy for Will to spout excuses and decide there's so much he just can't do. After all, he has cystic fibrosis. Just breathing can be difficult for him, and maintaining his weight is a challenge.

But that's not how Will sees things.

Will sees his hourlong treatments that clear mucus from his lungs and digestive system each morning and night as a pathway to being a top student and playing basketball, volleyball, baseball and golf.


He sees his cystic fibrosis not as a disease that will keep him from life, but as a chance to help others live a better life.


Christie Willhite Daily Herald
